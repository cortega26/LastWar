Resource Management

Resources fuel everything you do in Last War: Survival – from constructing buildings to recruiting heroes and powering your base through seasonal storms. This page summarises the various resources and how to obtain them efficiently.

Basic Resources

The core resources are food, gold and iron. You produce these in your farmland, gold mine and iron mine respectively; upgrade these buildings regularly to increase output. You can also gather from resource tiles on the world map, attack zombie hordes and plunder armored trucks for large hauls. Keep a reserve in your vault to prevent enemies from stealing everything during raids【544682748183740†L190-L195】.

Special Resources

  • Immune proteins & mutated crystals: Seasonal resources used in The Crimson Plague. Produce proteins in your protein farm and earn crystals by killing zombies, vaccinating infected members and completing seasonal missions【522118572126250†L45-L139】.
  • Titanium alloy, coal & rare earth: Introduced in Polar Storm, titanium is made in titanium factories and coal is harvested from excavation sites, zombie bosses and radar missions. Coal fuels furnaces to warm your base while rare earths drop from owning cities and determine your season ranking【1573696249107†L167-L199】.

Shards & Drone Parts

Hero shards are used to recruit and upgrade heroes. Generic gold shards can be spent on any hero, while specific shards are tied to a particular character. You can acquire shards from many sources: winning Warzone Capitol fights (five shards), refreshing secret missions (up to seven per day), the campaign store (ten per week), the honor store (ten per month), the VIP store from level 11 onward (seven per week), the alliance store (five per week) and reward chests【544682748183740†L92-L132】.  Dispatched trucks yield eight to twelve shards per run, trains may drop four shards and completing eighteen Arms Race goals awards two extra shards daily【544682748183740†L92-L132】.

Drone parts level up your combat drone. You can earn up to six parts per day from daily task chests. Drone missions and radar missions can each drop up to fifteen parts if you hit the multiplier【544682748183740†L134-L146】.

Diamonds & Premium Currency

Diamonds are the premium currency. F2P players can still earn a fair amount: daily tasks and chests yield up to 110 diamonds; alliance tech donations grant 30 per day; free mall chests provide 10 per day; Warzone Capitol chests drop up to 700; clicking thumbs in the arena’s 3v3 brawl awards 90 per day; Duel VS tiers give 500–2 000 per week depending on rank; radar missions sometimes reveal diamond mines worth up to 100; digging treasures has a chance to double that value; and the Marshall exercise offers diamonds several times a week【544682748183740†L150-L175】.

Resource Safety

Whenever possible, leave resources in the form of boxes in your inventory rather than opening them immediately. Unopened resources cannot be plundered by other commanders if they break through your walls【544682748183740†L190-L195】. Use vaults to protect a portion of your resources and shield your base during PvP events. Farming alt accounts or plundering trucks in other servers are effective ways to boost your reserves【705118852602196†L43-L100】.
