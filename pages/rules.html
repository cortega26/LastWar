HxC Rules

Guidelines to keep our alliance strong and organized.

  1. Be always polite and respectful.
  2. Be active (specially if you're F2P).
  3. Donate to alliance tech everyday.
  4. Meet the required minimums of the alliance.
  5. During Zombie Invasion (golden zombies): if you can't tank a boss, don't rally it.
  6. Be disciplined and organized: learn how to place your base in a grid, aligned with other bases.
  7. Participate in Events: Marshall's Guard, Desert Storm, Winter Storm, Capitol War, SVS and any other relevant event introduced in the game.
