Season 1 Black Market Guide

Specially designed for F2P and low spenders

How to Earn Black Market Cash

Total Available: 10,000+ BM Cash (F2P) over 4 weeks
Week 1: ~5,500 from Bingo Board
Weeks 2-4: Daily challenges + Champion Duel betting (5 free bets/day)
Key Strategy: Always bet on highest multipliers in Champion Duel - no risk of losing your principal.

What You Can Buy

Black Market store refreshes 8 times daily (first free). Each refresh offers mix of free, discounted, and regular items.

Tip: Roll all 8 times every day to see a different selection of items.

Exclusive Weapon Shards

Kimberly, Tesla and DVA shards. Buy these first if you don't already own their weapons.

3,000 BM Cash total

Legendary Gear Blueprints

Gold blueprints for gear upgrades. Essential for level 40+ equipment. Very rare in regular play.

4,000 BM Cash (5-pack)

Base Decorations

Tower of Victory and similar. Permanent army stat boosts. Upgrade to level 3 for max effect.

Variable pricing

Upgrade Materials

Skill medals, drone parts, upgrade ore. Often discounted. Good for bottlenecks.

60 BM Cash (discounted)

Purchase Priority (Most Important First)

1. Exclusive Weapon Shards for Kimberly, Tesla and DVA

Top priority purchase if you don't already own these exclusive weapons.

3,000 BM Cash per weapon

2. Legendary Gear Blueprints

Essential for late game. Benefits all heroes. Extremely rare otherwise.

4,000 BM Cash (5-pack)

3. Exclusive Decorations

Permanent base buffs. Only available to top players or spenders normally.

4. Upgrade Materials (If Needed)

Only buy discounted bundles for specific bottlenecks. Available through regular play.

5. Avoid: Common Items

Hero shards, resources, speed-ups. Poor value - get these free through daily play.

F2P Strategy Summary

Optimal Spending Plan

Budget: 9,000+ BM Cash

  • Get 1-2 exclusive weapons (6,000 cash)
  • Buy 1 legendary blueprint pack (4,000 cash) if available
  • Remaining cash: decorations or discounted materials
  • Never buy: hero shards, resources, speed-ups
Important: Mythic gear blueprints are NOT in Season 1 Black Market. These come from later events or paid content.

Why This Matters

Black Market gives F2P players access to items normally locked behind paywalls:

  • Season-exclusive weapons (originally $20-50+ packs)
  • Legendary blueprints (usually VIP store only)
  • Premium decorations (top 1% event rewards)
Smart Move: Ignore paid BM Cash packs. The free currency is enough for the best items.
