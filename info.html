RA Virus System

Impact on Gameplay

  • Base Effect: Reduces troop combat effectiveness by 20-50% depending on infection level
  • Scaling: Virus potency increases with zombie level and city-level disparity
  • Zone Effects: Higher infection rates in central map zones (up to 60% reduction)

Resistance Mechanics

  • Baseline Resistance: All players start with 15% resistance
  • Resistance Cap: Maximum achievable resistance is 95%
  • Diminishing Returns: Resistance upgrades become less effective after 65%
  • Alliance Bonus: Each member with 50%+ resistance adds 0.5% to alliance resistance

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the optimal early-game strategy for F2P players?

For F2P players, focus on rapid development of your Protein Farm (to level 10) and Virus Research Institute (to level 5) within the first 5 days. Join an active alliance immediately and prioritize virus resistance research over combat power initially. Resource gathering efficiency and targeted speedup usage are critical for maintaining competitive progression.

How does the RA Virus resistance work exactly?

RA Virus resistance is a percentage value that directly counters the negative effects of zone infection. For example, if you're in a zone with 40% infection level and you have 60% resistance, the effective debuff is only 16% (40% √ó (1 - 0.6)). Resistance is gained through specialized buildings, research, hero abilities, equipment, and alliance bonuses. The maximum theoretical resistance is capped at 95%.

Which profession is better, Engineer or Warrior?

Neither profession is universally better - they serve different strategic roles. Engineers excel at resource production, research acceleration, and infrastructure development, making them vital for alliance growth. Warriors excel at combat operations, territory control, and elite hunting. For individual players, the choice should match your playstyle. For alliances, a balanced composition of approximately 40% Engineers and 60% Warriors is recommended for optimal performance.

How important is alliance membership in Season 1?

Alliance membership is critically important in Season 1 due to the virus resistance sharing mechanics and specialized role requirements. Active alliances provide resource assistance, strategic coordination, territory protection, and resistance bonuses. The collective alliance resistance bonus can contribute up to 10% additional resistance to all members, which is a significant advantage in higher infection zones. Solo play is extremely challenging in this season and not recommended for competitive progress.

What's the best way to increase virus resistance quickly?

The fastest path to increased virus resistance is a multi-faceted approach:

  1. Prioritize Virus Research Institute upgrades to at least level 10
  2. Focus research on the "Viral Immunity" tech tree
  3. Recruit and level heroes with resistance bonuses (Dr. Morgan, Medic Chen)
  4. Collect and equip specialized anti-viral gear sets
  5. Join an active alliance with high collective resistance
For a major resistance boost, complete the "Viral Adaptation" event series which occurs weekly, providing up to 3% permanent resistance upon completion.

How do I effectively counter players with higher resistance?

When facing opponents with higher virus resistance, consider these strategies:

  • Deploy specialized viral agent units that can bypass 15% of enemy resistance
  • Research and employ resistance penetration technologies
  • Use heroes with abilities that target resistance mechanics
  • Coordinate with alliance members for multi-pronged attacks
  • Focus on attacking during times when zone infection levels are higher (typically during server events)
Remember that resistance advantages diminish in direct proportion to the gap - a 10% resistance advantage typically translates to only a 3-5% combat advantage.

What happens to my progress after Season 1 ends?

At the conclusion of Season 1, players receive season rewards based on individual and alliance rankings. A portion of your development converts to permanent account benefits:

  • 10% of your maximum achieved virus resistance becomes a permanent resistance bonus
  • Special season heroes remain in your roster with slightly reduced abilities
  • Season-specific research provides partial permanent bonuses to related technologies
  • Season achievement badges provide permanent passive bonuses
These benefits give returning players an advantage in future seasons while maintaining balance for new players.
