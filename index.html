Last War Survival Companion

Welcome to your all‑in‑one resource for Last War: Survival. This community‑driven site compiles guides, strategies and calculators to help both new and veteran commanders thrive in the zombie‑infested wasteland. Explore the sections below to learn how to build your base, join alliances, master seasonal mechanics, manage resources and uncover hidden tricks.

Explore the Guides

  • Base Building – Learn about every building type, when to construct them and tips for maximising your construction speed.
  • Alliances – Discover the benefits of joining an alliance, how to contribute and participate in major alliance events.
  • Seasons – Detailed breakdowns of Season 1: The Crimson Plague and Season 2: Polar Storm, including new resources, buildings and strategies.
  • Resources – A guide to obtaining and safeguarding food, gold, shards, diamonds, mutated crystals, coal and more.
  • Events – Everything you need to know about secret missions, Ghost Ops, Operation Falcon, world bosses and other special modes.
  • Tips & Tricks – Hidden strategies and quality‑of‑life tricks that aren’t obvious but make a big difference.

Use the navigation bar at the top to access these pages from anywhere on the site. New content will be added as further seasons and events appear in the game. If you have feedback or wish to contribute, please open an issue in the project repository.
